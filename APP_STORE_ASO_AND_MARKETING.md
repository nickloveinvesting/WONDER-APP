### 1.1 Keyword Analysis Framework

**Three Dimensions to Consider:**

1. **Search Volume:** How many people search for this term monthly
2. **Competition:** How many apps already target this keyword
3. **Relevance:** How well does WONDER match the intent

**Scoring System (1-5):**
- Volume: 5 = 10K+/month, 1 = <1K/month
- Competition: 5 = Very high, 1 = Very low  
- Relevance: 5 = Perfect match, 1 = Poor match
