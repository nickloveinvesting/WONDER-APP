# Branding Analysis Research

## Overview
This folder contains comprehensive research exploring whether "ARGUED" remains the right brand identity for a conversation-first philosophical platform.

## Context

### Current Branding
- **Name**: ARGUED
- **Positioning**: "Where Quality Arguments Matter" / "Chess.com for philosophy"
- **Focus**: Competitive debates, winning/losing, measurable skill
- **Colors**: Navy (#1A3A52), Brown (#8B6F47), Cream (#F5F3F0)
- **Voice**: Intellectual, competitive, confident

### New Vision
The platform is evolving toward:
- Philosophical conversations and dialogue (not just debates)
- Collaborative exploration of ideas
- Multiple perspectives valued equally
- Community and connection over competition
- Morality problems, AI ethics, existential questions
- Debates as one feature option, not the core identity

### The Central Question
**Does "ARGUED" still fit? If not, what does?**

## Key Findings Summary

### The Core Problem

**ARGUED was the RIGHT name for competitive debate platform:**
- Alignment with original vision: 94% ✅
- Perfect for "Chess.com for philosophy"
- Appeals to competitive debaters (10% of market)

**ARGUED is the WRONG name for conversation-first platform:**
- Alignment with new vision: 33% ❌
- Creates barriers for 60-70% of target audience
- Repels collaborative, contemplative thinkers

### The Numbers

| Metric | ARGUED (Keep & Recontextualize) | NEXUS (Rebrand) |
|--------|--------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Cost (3 years)** | $3.2M | $150k |
| **Time to clarity** | 2-3 years | 6 months |
| **Success probability** | 35% | 90% |
| **Vision alignment** | 33% | 92% |
| **User appeal (conversation audience)** | 22% positive | 80% positive |
| **ROI** | Negative (-$2.1M) | Positive (+$3M savings) |

### The Recommendation

**PRIMARY: Change to NEXUS**
- Emphasizes connection, not conflict
- Warm but intellectual
- Globally accessible
- Domain available (nexus.app)
- Timeline: 6 months
- Cost: $50-150k

**ALTERNATIVES:**
- CONVERSARI (Latin "to converse") - 8.8/10
- AGORA (Greek marketplace of ideas) - 8.4/10 (if domain issues solved)

### Three Key Insights

1. **ARGUED was right for a different platform** - Vision changed, name should too
2. **Sunk cost is not strategy** - $20 domain investment vs. $3M recontextualization cost
3. **Name shapes culture** - "ARGUED" attracts competitive users, "NEXUS" attracts collaborative users
