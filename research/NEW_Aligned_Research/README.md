## Why This Research Matters

The original research in `/research/` excellently solved for a competitive debate platform. It was **not wrong** - it was perfectly aligned with that vision. However, we're now building something fundamentally different:

### A Platform for Dialogue, Not Victory

We're creating a space where:
- Multiple perspectives are valued, not judged as "right" or "wrong"
- Success is measured by depth of understanding, not wins
- AI helps facilitate conversations, not declare winners
- Community forms around shared inquiry, not competition
- Engagement comes from curiosity, not leaderboards
