# Launch Platform Priority & Timeline

## Last Updated: 2025-11-14

This document outlines the strategic timeline for promoting ARGUED across different platforms, prioritized by impact, effort, and readiness requirements.

---

## Launch Strategy Overview

### Three-Phase Approach

**Phase 1: Day 1 - Launch Week** (Maximum Impact)
- Focus: High-traffic platforms that allow launch announcements
- Goal: Initial user acquisition and momentum
- Target: 1,000-3,000 signups

**Phase 2: Week 1-4** (Community Building)
- Focus: Community engagement and word-of-mouth
- Goal: Establish presence in philosophy communities
- Target: 2,000-5,000 additional signups

**Phase 3: Month 1-3** (Sustained Growth)
- Focus: Long-term engagement and credibility
- Goal: Build reputation and organic growth
- Target: Steady 100-200 signups/week

## Rationale for Prioritization

### Why This Order?

**Day 1 Focus on High-Traffic Platforms**:
- Product Hunt and Hacker News have highest traffic potential
- Create immediate momentum
- Validate product-market fit
- Generate social proof for other platforms

**Week 1 Focus on Communities**:
- Build presence in target communities
- Engage directly with ideal users
- Gather feedback for improvement
- Create word-of-mouth foundation

**Month 1+ Focus on Sustainability**:
- Content marketing for SEO and authority
- Community building for retention
- Partnerships for credibility
- Optimization for efficiency

**Long-term Focus on Scaling**:
- Academic partnerships for stability
- Influencers for reach
- International for growth
- Paid channels for predictability

## Adjustment Strategy

### If Launch Week Underperforms:
- Double down on community engagement
- Gather more feedback
- Improve product before next push
- Focus on quality over quantity

### If Launch Week Exceeds Expectations:
- Accelerate content marketing
- Increase community support capacity
- Consider paid advertising sooner
- Scale up partnership outreach

### Monthly Review:
- Analyze which platforms drove highest quality users
- Identify most efficient channels
- Cut low-performing platforms
- Double down on working channels
- Adjust timeline based on learnings
