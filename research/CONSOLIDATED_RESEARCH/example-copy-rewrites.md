# Example Copy Rewrites: From Competitive to Conversation-First

**Document**: Before/After Copy Examples
**Date**: November 14, 2025
**Purpose**: Demonstrate voice shift from competitive debate to collaborative exploration

---

## How to Use This Document

Each section shows:
- **BEFORE**: Current competitive/debate framing (ARGUED)
- **AFTER**: Conversation-first framing (Sage-Explorer voice)
- **Why Changed**: Reasoning behind the revision

---

## 1. Homepage Headlines

### Hero Headline

**BEFORE:**
> # ARGUED: Where Quality Arguments Matter

**AFTER:**
> # Where Curious Minds Explore Philosophy Together

**Why Changed:**
- "ARGUED" is the platform name (debate-focused)
- "Arguments" emphasizes competition and performance
- "Quality" implies judgment and scoring
- New version emphasizes curiosity, collaboration, and exploration

**Alternative Options:**
> # Philosophy Happens in Conversation
> # Ask Deep Questions. Think Together.
> # Where Thoughtful People Explore Big Ideas

---

### Hero Subheadline

**BEFORE:**
> Compete with philosophers worldwide. Improve your argumentation skills. Build your DeLO rating.

**AFTER:**
> Ask deep questions. Explore diverse perspectives. Think more clearly about what matters.

**Why Changed:**
- Removes "compete" (zero-sum framing)
- Removes "DeLO rating" (performance metric)
- Emphasizes questions (entry point) over skills (performance)
- "What matters" is more personal/meaningful than "argumentation skills"

**Alternative Options:**
> Philosophy for everyone who wonders why. Explore questions that matter with a community of thoughtful people.

> Wonder together. Reason carefully. Discover perspectives you hadn't considered.

---

### Value Proposition

**BEFORE:**
> We exist to make philosophical thinking accessible, competitive, and rewarding. We reject the false binary of academic exclusivity versus social media chaos. ARGUED creates a middle ground: a structured intellectual space where argumentation quality is celebrated and measurable progress is real.

**AFTER:**
> Philosophy begins with curiosity about the world and our place in it. We're a structured space for exploring big questions togetherâ€”free from academic gatekeeping and social media chaos. Here, diverse perspectives strengthen understanding, and thoughtful conversation leads to clearer thinking.

**Why Changed:**
- Opens with curiosity (invitation) not competition
- Removes "measurable progress" (performance metric)
- Changes "argumentation quality" to "thoughtful conversation"
- Emphasizes diverse perspectives and collective understanding
- Keeps "structured" to differentiate from casual forums

> - Evidence (0-100): Quality and relevance of citations
> - Rhetoric (0-100): Persuasiveness and framing

## Summary: Before/After Voice Shift

| Element | Before (ARGUED) | After (Conversation-First) |
|---------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Core Metaphor** | Chess match, battlefield | Seminar, exploration |
| **User Role** | Competitor, debater | Curious thinker, explorer |
| **AI Role** | Judge, evaluator | Guide, facilitator |
| **Success Metric** | Winning, rating | Understanding, connection |
| **Emotional Tone** | Challenged, competitive | Curious, welcomed |
| **Primary Action** | Argue, defend, attack | Explore, consider, wonder |
| **Community Bond** | Rankings, competition | Shared curiosity, diverse perspectives |
| **Mistakes Are** | Losses, failures | Learning opportunities |
| **Outcome** | Winner/loser | Deeper understanding |
