## ALTERNATIVE DOMAIN STRATEGIES

If primary domains unavailable, consider:

### Strategy 1: .app First Approach
**Embrace .app as primary domain**
- Modern, app-appropriate extension
- Google-backed, secure, trustworthy
- Cost-effective acquisition
- Example: **ponder.app** as primary

**Advantages:**
- Affordable ($20-500 typically)
- Modern, tech-forward
- Clear that it's an application
- No need for "get" or "app" modifier

**Disadvantages:**
- Less familiar than .com
- Some users default to .com
- Need to educate market

### Strategy 2: Modifier Approach
**Add descriptive modifier to get clean .com**

**Options:**
- get[name].com (getponder.com)
- [name]app.com (ponderapp.com)
- [name]together.com (pondertogether.com)
- join[name].com (joinponder.com)

**Best modifiers:**
- **"get"** - Modern, action-oriented (getponder.com)
- **"join"** - Community-focused (joinparley.com)
- **[name]app"** - Clear that it's an app (ponderapp.com)

**Avoid:**
- "my" (dated: myponder.com)
- "the" (awkward: theponder.com)
- "try" (temporary feeling: tryponder.com)

### Strategy 3: Compound Word
**Create unique compound unavailable elsewhere**

**Examples:**
- **pondertogether.com** - Available, clear
- **wondermind.com** - Might be available
- **sparkconverse.com** - Likely available

**Advantages:**
- Unique, ownable
- Descriptive
- Available

**Disadvantages:**
- Longer, harder to remember
- Awkward to say
- Dilutes brand simplicity
