# Name Scoring Matrix
**Research Date:** November 14, 2025
**Agent:** Agent 2 - Alternative Name Generation & Evaluation
**Methodology:** Top 20 conversation-first names scored on 6 key criteria

---

## Scoring Criteria (1-10 scale)

### 1. Conversation-First Alignment
**Question:** How well does this name emphasize dialogue, exploration, and collaboration over competition?
- 10 = Perfect fit for "where philosophical minds meet"
- 1 = Emphasizes competition/debate over conversation

### 2. Memorability
**Question:** How easy is this name to remember and recall?
- 10 = Instantly memorable, simple, sticky
- 1 = Forgettable, complex, hard to recall

### 3. Virality Potential (Verb-ability)
**Question:** Does it work as a verb? Can users say "Let's ____ this"?
- 10 = Perfect verb, natural usage, viral potential
- 1 = Cannot be verbed, no viral mechanics

### 4. Accessibility (Non-intimidating)
**Question:** Does it feel inclusive and welcoming vs. elitist?
- 10 = Extremely accessible, anyone can use it
- 1 = Intimidating, gatekeeping, academic only

### 5. Uniqueness
**Question:** Is it distinctive enough to own and stand out?
- 10 = Completely unique, no conflicts
- 1 = Generic, many similar products exist

### 6. International Appeal
**Question:** Does it work globally across languages and cultures?
- 10 = Universal, works everywhere
- 1 = English-specific, cultural barriers
