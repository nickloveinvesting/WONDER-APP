## 8. Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### Risk: iOS Freelancer Delivers Poor Quality
**Mitigation:**
- Vet carefully (App Store portfolio required)
- Milestone payments (40% simulator, 60% completion)
- 2-week support in contract
**Contingency:** Budget $300 for backup developer

### Risk: Infrastructure Costs Spike
**Mitigation:**
- Free tiers sufficient for 1K-10K users
- Monitor usage weekly
- Optimize queries before scaling
**Contingency:** Supabase Pro $25/month, Vercel Pro $20/month (absorbed by revenue)

### Risk: Budget Overruns
**Mitigation:**
- Add 20-30% buffer to all estimates
- Fixed-price contracts (not hourly)
- Track spending weekly
**Contingency:** Cut scope (not quality), delay iOS to Year 2, seek $5K-10K angel investment

### Total Contingency Buffer: $1,000
- Freelancer replacement: $300
- iOS rejection fixes: $200
- Infrastructure scaling: $500
