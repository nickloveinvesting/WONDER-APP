## 1. THE PHILOSOPHICAL RATING SYSTEM (DeLO)

### Inspired by: Chess ELO System

**Chess.com Success**:
- Clear skill measurement (800-2800+ rating)
- Every game affects rating
- Transparent calculations
- Separate ratings for different formats
- Provides matchmaking guidance

### PhiloDuel Rating System: **"DeLO" (Debate ELO)**

#### Overall DeLO Rating (Primary)
**Range**: 800-2800+
- **800-1000**: Novice Philosopher
- **1000-1200**: Apprentice Thinker
- **1200-1400**: Competent Debater
- **1400-1600**: Skilled Philosopher
- **1600-1800**: Advanced Dialectician
- **1800-2000**: Master Philosopher
- **2000-2200**: Grandmaster Thinker
- **2200+**: Philosopher King/Queen

#### Category-Specific Ratings
Users have separate ratings for philosophical domains:
- **Ethics DeLO** (moral philosophy debates)
- **Logic DeLO** (formal reasoning)
- **Metaphysics DeLO** (nature of reality)
- **Epistemology DeLO** (knowledge & belief)
- **Political Philosophy DeLO** (justice & governance)
- **Aesthetics DeLO** (art & beauty)

**Why Separate Ratings?**
- Experts in one area may be novices in another
- Allows specialized reputation building
- Encourages exploration of new areas
- More accurate matchmaking

### Rating Calculation Factors

#### **Win/Loss Record** (40% weight)
- Formal debate victories
- Judged by AI + community
- Peer matchups (beating higher-rated opponent = bigger gain)

#### **Argument Quality** (30% weight)
- Upvote ratio
- Citation frequency
- Logical fallacy detection (penalize)
- Steel-manning recognition (reward)
- AI quality assessment

#### **Community Impact** (20% weight)
- "Changed My View" instances
- Argument saves/shares
- Follow count growth
- Award frequency

#### **Participation & Consistency** (10% weight)
- Debate completion rate
- Response speed
- Engagement frequency

### Rating Transparency

**Public Information**:
- Overall DeLO rating
- Category-specific ratings
- Win/Loss record
- Peak rating (all-time high)
- Rating progression graph

**Private Information** (User Only):
- Detailed breakdown of rating factors
- Predicted rating change for potential debates
- Strengths/weaknesses analysis

### Rating Protection Mechanisms

**Anti-Gaming Measures**:
- Can't debate the same person repeatedly (rating manipulation)
- Suspiciously high win rates trigger review
- Sock puppet detection (multiple accounts)
- AI detection for plagiarized arguments
- Community reporting for bad faith debates

**Provisional Ratings** (First 20 Debates):
- Higher volatility (Â±50 points per debate)
- Marked with (P) next to rating
- Allows system to find true skill level quickly

**Rating Floors** (Optional):
- Users can't drop below their monthly peak minus 100 points
- Prevents "rating anxiety" paralysis
- Encourages continued participation
