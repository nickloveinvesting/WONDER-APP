## 7. PREVENTING FAILURE MODES

### Problem: Echo Chambers

**Symptoms**:
- Only one view dominates in a space
- Dissent downvoted into oblivion
- Groupthink replaces critical thinking

**Solutions** (2025 research-backed):
1. **Algorithmic Diversity**: Feed shows opposing viewpoints (not just agreement)
2. **Devil's Advocate Required**: Popular positions must face steel-manned opposition
3. **Cross-School Pollination**: Recommendations from different schools
4. **Ideological Diversity Score**: Track and reward exposure to different views
5. **"Changed My View" Emphasis**: Changing minds valued over "winning"

**Research Insight**: C. Thi Nguyen (2025) - echo chambers aren't sealed; opposing views arrive "pre-discredited." Solution: Direct engagement with strongest opposing arguments.

---

### Problem: Toxic Behavior

**Symptoms**:
- Personal attacks (ad hominem)
- Bad faith arguments
- Trolling and derailment
- Harassment

**Solutions**:
1. **Good Faith Score**: AI + community track argumentative integrity
2. **Three-Strike System**: Warning â†’ Temp ban â†’ Permanent ban
3. **Reputation Gates**: Trolls can't access quality spaces
4. **Community Muting**: High-rep users can silence bad actors
5. **Transparent Moderation Logs**: All mod actions visible

---

### Problem: Expert Gatekeeping

**Symptoms**:
- Jargon exclusion
- Condescension to newcomers
- "Real philosophers only" attitude

**Solutions**:
1. **Philosophy 101 Spaces**: Beginner-only areas (no shaming)
2. **Glossary Integration**: Hover over terms for definitions
3. **"Helpful to Newcomers" Badge**: Reward accessibility
4. **ELI5 Requirements**: Advanced debates must have plain-language summaries
5. **Accessibility Score**: Arguments rated for understandability

---

### Problem: Low-Quality Content

**Symptoms**:
- One-sentence "arguments"
- No sources or reasoning
- Memes over substance

**Solutions**:
1. **Separate Spaces**: Casual philosophy vs. rigorous debate
2. **Character Minimums**: Advanced rooms require 200+ character arguments
3. **Citation Requirements**: Factual claims need sources
4. **Upvote Ratio Filters**: Low-quality content hidden automatically
5. **"Substantive Argument" Badge**: Reward depth
