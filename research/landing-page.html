Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PhiloDuel

Isn't this only for philosophy experts?
‚ñº

Absolutely not. PhiloDuel is designed for everyone from curious beginners to professionals. We have beginner-friendly categories, guided learning, and community support. Everyone starts somewhere.

How does AI judging work? Can it be biased?
‚ñº

Our AI is trained on thousands of philosophical arguments, evaluated by human experts. It scores on logic, evidence, clarity, and persuasiveness. We publish our methodology and get external audits regularly. It's more fair than popularity voting.

Can I remain anonymous?
‚ñº

Yes. You can use any username and never connect it to your real identity. Your philosophical ideas matter more than your credentials.

Will this become toxic like other debate platforms?
‚ñº

We have strict guidelines: ad hominem attacks get moderated, not arguments. Bad-faith actors get warnings then removed. School of Thought communities are self-moderated by their members. Quality is enforced systematically.

‚ñº

We're launching with a fully responsive web app first (works great on mobile). Native iOS and Android apps are coming in Q2 2025. Early access members get priority beta access.
