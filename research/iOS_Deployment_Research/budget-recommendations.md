# Budget Recommendations for iOS Deployment

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

## Executive Summary

**Minimum viable budget:** $159 (DIY everything)
**Recommended budget:** $1,000-1,500 (hybrid approach)

```

Phase 3 (Month 4+): Invest in design polish
         Cost: $300-500

TOTAL: $660-1,060 spread over time
vs. $1,500 upfront
```

```

✓ TestFlight: Free beta testing
✓ Canva: Free design tools for icons
✓ PrivacyPolicy.com: Free policy generator

SAVINGS: $300-500
```

## BUDGETING RED FLAGS

### When Budget is Too Low
❌ **Under $500 and non-technical**
- You'll struggle to get quality help
- May end up spending more fixing mistakes
- Consider waiting to save more

❌ **Expecting agency quality for <$2,000**
- Unrealistic expectations
- Will lead to disappointment
- Better to DIY or hire freelancer

---

### When Budget is Too High
❌ **Spending $10,000+ on first version**
- Overkill for MVP
- Build simple first, invest more later
- Unless you're enterprise/well-funded

---

### Budget Allocation Mistakes
❌ **All money on development, none on design**
- App will look unprofessional
- Reserve 10-15% for design/assets

❌ **No buffer for unexpected costs**
- Always budget 15-25% buffer
- Unexpected: re-submissions, fixes, updates

❌ **Forgetting ongoing costs**
- Budget for Year 1, not just launch
- Include $300-1,000 for maintenance

## BREAK-EVEN CALCULATIONS

### DIY vs Hire Freelancer

```
DIY Cost: $159
DIY Time: 60 hours

Hire Cost: $859 (with freelancer)
Hire Time: 25 hours

Difference: $700 for 35 hours saved
Hourly rate: $20/hour

CONCLUSION: If your time is worth >$20/hr, hire help
```

---

### Hire Freelancer vs Agency

```
Freelancer Cost: $1,289 (Year 1)
Freelancer Time: 30 hours

Agency Cost: $6,959 (Year 1)
Agency Time: 15 hours

Difference: $5,670 for 15 hours saved
Hourly rate: $378/hour

CONCLUSION: Only worth it if your time is worth >$378/hr
OR if quality/speed justifies premium
```

---

### Cloud Build vs Buy Mac

```
Mac Mini Cost: $600 upfront
Cloud Builds: $30/month = $360/year

Year 1: Cloud cheaper ($360 < $600)
Year 2: Mac cheaper ($0 < $360)
Break-even: 20 months

CONCLUSION:
- Buy Mac if you'll use 2+ years AND use Mac for other work
- Use cloud if iOS app is only use case
```
