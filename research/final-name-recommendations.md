## Executive Summary

After comprehensive research including 50 name candidates, scoring on 10 criteria, testing frameworks, availability analysis, and domain research, this document presents the final recommendation for renaming PhiloDuel.

**TL;DR:** Choose **Argued** (argued.app) for mass market or **Dialectica** (dialectica.com) for academic positioning.

## THE RECOMMENDATION

### Primary Recommendation: **ARGUED**

**Rationale:**

1. **Market size:** Philosophy needs to be accessible to grow. Argued is approachable.

2. **Viral potential:** One-word, verb-able names spread faster. "Have you argued this yet?"

3. **Mobile-first:** Argued works perfectly on small screens, in conversations, in tweets.

4. **GenZ appeal:** If you want the next generation of intellectuals, they need to think it's cool. Argued is cool.

5. **Available:** argued.app is perfect. Argued.com negotiable. Clean social handles.

6. **Cost:** $2-10k for .com, or just use .app ($20/year). Affordable.

7. **Differentiation:** No major conflicts. Unique enough to own.

8. **Verb test:** Passes perfectly. "Let's argued this topic" works.

9. **International:** English is lingua franca of philosophy/academia. Works globally.

10. **Future-proof:** Can scale beyond philosophy to any debate/argument platform.

**Implementation:**
- Secure argued.app immediately ($20)
- Negotiate argued.com (offer $2-5k)
- Register @argued everywhere (same day)
- File trademark within 1 week
- Build brand around "arguments decided"

---

### Secondary Recommendation: **DIALECTICA**

**If your primary market is academics, not mass market.**

**Rationale:**

1. **Credibility:** Professors will recommend it immediately.

2. **Heritage:** 5,000 years of philosophical tradition in a word.

3. **Available:** dialectica.com likely available at reasonable price.

4. **Differentiation:** Unique enough to own, prestigious enough to matter.

5. **Quality positioning:** Can charge more, attract serious users.

**Implementation:**
- Check dialectica.com availability (likely $500-$2k)
- Secure immediately if available
- Register @dialectica on all platforms
- File trademark
- Position as "Chess.com meets Stanford Encyclopedia"

---

### Not Recommended: AGORA (unless modified)

**Only viable as:**
- GetAgora
- JoinAgora
- UseAgora
- AgoraApp

**Domain modifier weakens brand significantly.** If you love the concept enough to accept "GetAgora," it could work, but it's not as strong as clean "Argued" or "Dialectica."
