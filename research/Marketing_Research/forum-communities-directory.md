## Philosophy-Specific Forums

### 1. Philosophy Stack Exchange
- **URL**: https://philosophy.stackexchange.com/
- **Community Size**: ~35,000 users, ~15,000 questions
- **Activity Level**: High - 20-30 new questions daily
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - Self-promotion strictly limited
  - Must provide value in answers first
  - Can mention tools in profile/bio
  - Link only when directly relevant to answering questions
  - Spam is aggressively moderated
- **Best Content Format**:
  - Answer philosophy questions thoroughly
  - Link to ARGUED only when directly solving the questioner's problem
  - Build reputation (500+) before any promotional activity
- **Culture**: Academic, strict quality standards, evidence-based
- **Contact**: Community moderators via site flags
- **Notes**: Stack Exchange has strict anti-spam policies. Best approach is genuine participation with subtle mentions.

### 2. The Philosophy Forum
- **URL**: https://thephilosophyforum.com/
- **Community Size**: ~5,000+ active members
- **Activity Level**: High - dozens of active discussions daily
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - Introductory posts allowed in "Lounge"
  - Must engage authentically before promoting
  - Self-promotion threads are acceptable if adding value
  - No spam in discussion threads
- **Best Content Format**:
  - Start genuine philosophical discussions
  - Introduce tool in "Introduce Yourself" section
  - Create a "Show and Tell" thread
- **Culture**: Welcoming, diverse philosophical backgrounds, beginner-friendly
- **Contact**: Admin contact through site contact form
- **Notes**: More lenient than Stack Exchange. Good for beta testers recruitment.

### 3. Philosophy Now Forum
- **URL**: https://philosophynow.org/
- **Community Size**: ~10,000 readers, smaller forum community
- **Activity Level**: Medium - magazine-centric
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - Limited forum functionality
  - Focus on article comments
  - Contact for partnership/advertising opportunities
- **Best Content Format**:
  - Thoughtful comments on articles
  - Email editor for guest posts or partnerships
- **Culture**: Accessible philosophy, public intellectuals
- **Contact**: editor@philosophynow.org
- **Notes**: Better for content marketing than direct promotion. Consider guest articles.

### 4. Daily Nous
- **URL**: https://dailynous.com/
- **Community Size**: ~20,000+ daily readers
- **Activity Level**: Very High - multiple posts daily
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - Academic/professional focus
  - "You might also like" submissions accepted
  - Must be relevant to professional philosophy
  - High-quality threshold
- **Best Content Format**:
  - Submit as a new tool for philosophers
  - Comment on relevant discussions
  - Target "Resources for Philosophers" features
- **Culture**: Academic, professional philosophers, serious discussions
- **Contact**: justin.weinberg@gmail.com (founder)
- **Notes**: Highly influential in academic philosophy. One feature here = significant traffic.

### 5. Quora - Philosophy Topics
- **URL**: https://www.quora.com/topic/Philosophy
- **Community Size**: Millions of users, thousands active in philosophy
- **Activity Level**: Very High - hundreds of questions daily
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - Answer questions genuinely
  - Can mention your product if relevant
  - Profile can link to projects
  - Avoid pure promotional answers
  - Quality threshold enforced by community
- **Best Content Format**:
  - Comprehensive answers to philosophy questions
  - Natural mention of ARGUED when solving discussion problems
  - Use Spaces feature to build community
- **Culture**: Mixed - from beginners to experts, varied quality
- **Contact**: No central contact; community moderation
- **Notes**: High traffic potential. Create a Quora Space for ARGUED discussions.

### 6. Philosophy Overflow

## General Discussion Platforms

### 7. Hacker News
- **URL**: https://news.ycombinator.com/
- **Community Size**: ~500,000+ active users
- **Activity Level**: Extremely High
- **Promotion Rules**:
  - "Show HN" posts allowed for original work
  - Must be showing something you built
  - No marketing speak - be authentic
  - Engage with all comments
  - Don't vote manipulate
  - One Show HN per project
- **Best Content Format**:
  - "Show HN: ARGUED - A platform for philosophical discussions with AI facilitation"
  - Technical details welcome
  - Emphasize the "how" and "why" you built it
  - Sunday evenings (PST) often best for Show HN
- **Culture**: Technical, skeptical of marketing, values substance
- **Contact**: hn@ycombinator.com (for guideline questions only)
- **Notes**: Can drive massive traffic if HN community finds it interesting. Focus on technical innovation.
