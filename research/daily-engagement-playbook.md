## 8. INTELLECTUAL FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

### Creating Healthy FOMO

**Time-Limited Content**:
- Daily dilemma (gone after 24 hours)
- Live debate events (can't participate if you miss it)
- Tournament registration windows
- "Trending Now" debates (popularity window)

**Exclusive Access**:
- High-reputation users get early access to features
- Invitation-only philosophical salons
- Limited tournament spots (first 100 to register)

**Social Proof**:
- "5,000 philosophers are debating this right now"
- "You're missing the most upvoted argument of the day"
- "Your friends are active: Sarah, John, Mike"

**Achievement Windows**:
- Monthly leaderboard resets (last chance to hit top 10)
- Seasonal badges (only available this quarter)
- Event-specific awards (tournament winner badge)

### Balancing FOMO & Respect

**Good FOMO** (Motivating):
- ‚úÖ "Fascinating debate happening now"
- ‚úÖ "Today's dilemma is live"
- ‚úÖ "Tournament starts soon"

**Bad FOMO** (Manipulative):
- ‚ùå "Everyone is here except you"
- ‚ùå "You're falling behind"
- ‚ùå "Last chance or you'll regret it"

**Principle**: FOMO for intellectual opportunity, not social anxiety
