## NEGOTIATION STRATEGIES

### If Domain is Parked (For Sale)

**Research First:**
1. Use WHOIS to find owner
2. Check Wayback Machine (site history)
3. Look for "Make Offer" page
4. Research recent sale prices

**Negotiation Tips:**
1. Don't reveal your use case
2. Start low (40-60% of asking)
3. Be willing to walk away
4. Consider installment payments
5. Use domain broker if expensive

**Red Flags:**
- Asking price >$50k without traffic
- No history of use
- Professional domain flipper
- Unreasonable seller

### If Domain is In Use

**Evaluate:**
1. Is site active?
2. Is business viable?
3. Could you offer to buy business?
4. Would they sell just domain?

**Approach:**
1. Professional email via WHOIS contact
2. Offer fair price
3. Explain mutual benefit
4. Provide options (lease, installment)
5. Get attorney involved if serious

### Alternative: Lean Into Different TLD

**Modern approach:** .app, .ai are legitimate

**Examples:**
- Notion (notion.so, then .com)
- Figma (figma.com but started smaller)
- Linear (linear.app primary)

**Strategy:** Build brand first, buy .com later when profitable
