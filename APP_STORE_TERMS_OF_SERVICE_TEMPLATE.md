You agree NOT to:

**Hate Speech & Discrimination:**

**Bad Faith Arguments:**

- ❌ Gish gallop (overwhelming with weak arguments)

- ❌ Bad faith quotations (taking out of context)

- ❌ Multiple accounts to evade moderation

- ❌ Non-consensual intimate images

**Off-Topic & Spam:**

- ❌ Link spam or malicious URLs
- ❌ Excessive emoji/symbol spam
- ❌ Engagement bait

### C. Moderation
We reserve the right to:
- Remove content violating these guidelines
- Warn users for violations
- Temporarily suspend accounts (24 hours - 7 days)
- Permanently ban users for severe/repeated violations
- Report illegal content to authorities

**Progressive Discipline:**
1. **First offense:** Warning + explanation
2. **Second offense:** 24-hour mute
3. **Third offense:** 7-day suspension
4. **Repeated violations:** Permanent ban
5. **Severe violations** (harassment, hate speech): Immediate ban
