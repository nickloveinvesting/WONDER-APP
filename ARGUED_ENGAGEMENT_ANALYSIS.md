Based on research into successful platforms and the Hook Model (Nir Eyal), habit-forming products share these characteristics:

1. **Frequent engagement loops** (seconds to minutes, not days)
2. **Variable rewards** (unpredictable but desirable outcomes)
3. **Progressive investment** (users build value over time)
4. **External triggers** (notifications, emails, social pressure)
5. **Internal triggers** (solving emotional needs: boredom, loneliness, validation)

Let's analyze how leading platforms achieve this:
