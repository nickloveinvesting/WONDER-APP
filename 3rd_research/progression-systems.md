## 6. PROGRESSION PSYCHOLOGY

### **The "Level Up" Dopamine Hit**

**Triggers**:
- Rating milestone reached (1500! 1600! 1700!)
- Badge unlocked (notification + animation)
- Skill tree node unlocked (visual + access to new content)
- Persona mastery achieved (title granted)

**Maximize Impact**:
- Visual celebrations (confetti, animations)
- Sound effects (optional, tasteful)
- Social sharing ("I just hit 1600 DeLO!")
- Milestone rewards (bonus XP, cosmetic items)

---

### **The "Almost There" Effect**

**Examples**:
- "You're 50 DeLO points from Grandmaster!"
- "3 more debates to unlock Formal Logic"
- "2 more days to 30-day streak"
- "You're 85% to 'Persuasion Master' badge"

**Why It Works**:
- Creates urgency (almost there, don't stop now)
- Visible proximity to goals
- "Just one more" effect (one more debate to hit goal)

---

### **The "Mastery Feels Real" Effect**

**Traditional Problem**: Gamification feels hollow (arbitrary points)

**PhiloDuel Solution**: Progression reflects actual philosophical skill
- Higher rating = genuinely better arguments
- Badges = specific achievements (not just time spent)
- Skill trees = actual knowledge gained
- Community recognizes expertise

**Result**: Progression feels meaningful, not manipulative

## 8. ANTI-GAMING MEASURES

### **Progression Integrity**

**Prevent Rating Manipulation**:
- Can't repeatedly debate same low-rated opponent
- Suspicious win patterns flagged
- Sock puppet detection (multiple accounts)

**Prevent Badge Farming**:
- Badges require diverse achievements (can't spam one action)
- Time gates (can't unlock everything in one day)
- Quality thresholds (min upvote ratio required)

**Prevent XP Exploits**:
- Diminishing returns for repetitive actions
- Activity caps per day (prevents bots)
- Human verification for major milestones
