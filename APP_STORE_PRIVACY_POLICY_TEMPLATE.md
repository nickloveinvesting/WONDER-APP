## Introduction

WONDER ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our philosophical conversation platform, including our mobile application ("App") and related services.

**Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.**

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### A. Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration:**
- Email address (required for authentication)
- Username (public display name)
- Password (encrypted, we never see plain text)
- Profile information (optional): Display name, bio, profile picture, interests
- Expertise areas and learning interests

**Content You Create:**
- Debates (topics and descriptions)
- Arguments (your philosophical positions)
- Comments and discussions
- Reports and feedback
- Direct messages with other users (if available)

**Support Communications:**
- Emails you send to support@wonder.app
- In-app feedback submissions
- Bug reports and feature requests

**Payment Information (if applicable):**
- Billing name and address
- Payment method details (processed by secure payment providers, we don't store full credit card numbers)

### B. Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers
- Browser type and version
- IP address

**Usage Information:**
- Pages/sections you access and time spent
- Debates viewed and created
- Arguments submitted
- Features used
- Crashes and error logs
- App launch and exit times
- Network connection type (WiFi, cellular, etc.)

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location (country/region) based on IP address
- NOT precise GPS location (not requested)

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:**
- Session cookies (temporary, for maintaining login)
- Analytics cookies (understand usage patterns)
- Preference cookies (remember your settings)

**Important:** These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only as necessary to provide services.

### B. Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by:
- Court orders or legal process
- Government authorities
- Law enforcement investigations
- Protection of rights and safety

### C. Business Transfers
If WONDER is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the acquiring company (we'll notify you).

### D. Aggregated/De-identified Data
We may share aggregated statistics (e.g., "70% of debates are about ethics") with partners—this data cannot identify you.

### E. What We Do NOT Do
- ❌ We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
- ❌ We do NOT share your data for marketing purposes without consent
- ❌ We do NOT use your data to profile you or make automated decisions
- ❌ We do NOT provide data to advertisers for targeting

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Authentication:** Passwords are hashed and salted
- **Access Control:** Only authorized employees can access user data
- **Regular Audits:** Third-party security assessments
- **Incident Response:** Procedures in place for data breaches

### What You Should Know
- No system is 100% secure; we minimize risk but can't guarantee zero breaches
- You're responsible for keeping your password confidential
- Never share your account credentials

### Data Breach Notification
If a breach occurs affecting your data, we will notify you within 30 days via email or in-app notification, and comply with applicable laws.

## 6. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### A. Access Your Data
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Know what data we collect and why
- Understand how we use your data

**How to request:** Email support@wonder.app with "Data Access Request"

### B. Correct Your Data
You can update your profile information directly in Settings.

### C. Delete Your Data
You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data.

**How to request:**
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion (we'll process within 30 days)

**Important:** Deletion is permanent. We'll remove your personal data but may retain de-identified content for community benefit.

### D. Opt-Out of Communications
You can opt-out of non-essential communications:
- Unsubscribe from promotional emails (link in email)
- Disable notifications in app Settings
- Turn off analytics: Settings > Privacy

### E. Data Portability (GDPR)
You can request your data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

### F. Right to Restrict Processing
You can request we limit how we use your data while your request is reviewed.

### G. Object to Processing
You can object to certain types of processing (marketing, analytics).

**Response Time:** We respond to all data requests within 30 days.
